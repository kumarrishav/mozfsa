  • Student 'App of the Month'

    Each month, the Student Working Group will determine the theme for an app that would help improve or simplify the lives of students! These theme will be announced at the beginning of the month in our newsletter and Ambassadors will be asked to Submit their HTML5 apps that align with this theme (and have been submitted to the Firefox Marketplace) to be considered by the last day of the month.

    In addition to participating in the Mobilizer program or hosting a Hack Day, there are several easy ways in which you can promote Firefox OS on your campus.
    Spread Firefox OS online
    Let others at your university know about the opportunity to get involved by getting the word out over your website, blog, or social media. Please be sure and use the hash-tags used by your country's Mobilizer Group and include relevant hash-tags as they relate to your university!
