    If you are 18+ and currently associated with an institution of higher education, we would love to have you become a Firefox Student Ambassador!

    Join US

  • The Firefox Student Ambassadors provide an opportunity for those who love Firefox to:
    1. Obtain leadership roles on their campus and Help grow our global community (and spread our mission) around the world
    2. Receive a bunch of cool rewards and recognition opportunities
    3. Help promote the open web and gain marketing experience
    4. Potential for a Mozilla internship
    5. Access to a network of Firefox Student Ambassadors all over the world
    6. Potential visits to your campus by Mozilla staff.

  • Steps to Get Started as a Student Ambassador

    1. Complete the sign-up form
    2. Join our social networks
    3. Start a Firefox Club at your school (or join one that already exists)
    4. Get involved in an activity with your Firefox Club!

    We're here to help! Email firefoxstudents@mozilla.com with any questions you may have!
