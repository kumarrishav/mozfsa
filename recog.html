RECOGNITION

  • Level 1
    Firefox Student Ambassador Open Badge
    An open badge will be awarded to any Student Ambassador who completes the following tasks: 1. Officially registers for the program.
    2. Becomes familiar with Mozilla (completes Step 1 of the first 5 steps that an Ambassador should do).
    3. Does a Facebook/Twitter/Google+ post about the program using the hash-tags (#firefox #students #mozilla)
    4. Creates or joins a Firefox Club

    Recognition

  • Level 2
    Certificate of Identification
    A Certificate of identification as a Firefox Student Ambassador is available to each Ambassador who has already been awarded their open badge and completes one of the following actions:
    1. Makes a thimble or popcorn video about their favorite Mozilla project
    2. Writes a blog post about their experience in the program
    3. Hosts a Firefox Club event on their campus (and creates a page for their event that is posted on our Events Page)
    4. Attends an event as a representative of Mozilla

  • Level 3
    Vouching on Mozillians.org
    Being vouched on Mozillians.org can be achieved by a Firefox Student Ambassador if they have been awarded the open badge, certificate of identification, and accomplish the following tasks:
    1. Completed all five first program steps as outlined here
    2. Join our social networks
    Level 4
    Recommendation to the ReMo Program
    A Student Ambassador who meets the criteria below and is interested in joining the Mozilla Reps program, Then only apply for the ReMo program:

    Recogination

  • 1. Have completed Levels 1-3 in the Student Ambassador program
    2. Hold a leadership position within their Firefox Club
    3. Have been a member of the Firefox Student Ambassador program for at least ten months
    4. Have been vouched by a total of 3 Reps
