    Firefox Student Ambassador Blog

    Stay up-to-date about what's going on with the program on a regular basis on our blog

  • Social Media

    1. Share updates from the Student Ambassadors Facebook page on your Facebook profile.
    2. Follow @mozstudents on Twitter and send tweets with hashtag #firefoxstudents to ask questions
    3. Participate in the discussions happening on our Google+ Page ('Firefox Student Ambassadors')
    4. Tag project and event photos with #firefoxstudents on Flickr
    5. Follow ReMo guidelines for social media activism

    Student Ambassadors use IRC (internet relay chat) and the Mozilla forums to ask questions, share ideas, and interact with other Mozillians. Sign-on to IRC and join the #firefoxstudents channel.
